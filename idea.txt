Elevator Pitch

Every day, thousands of healthcare insurance claims are delayed, underpaid, or rejected—not because the treatment wasn't covered, but because the submitted documents contain inconsistencies, missing information, billing mistakes, or policy mismatches.

ClaimIQ is an AI-powered claim auditing platform that reviews an insurance claim before submission, detects potential issues, explains why they matter, estimates the likelihood of rejection, and recommends fixes.

Instead of replacing insurers or doctors, ClaimIQ acts as an intelligent quality-control layer between hospitals, patients, and insurance companies.

Why AI Is Necessary

Traditional software can check whether a document exists.

Only AI can understand:

whether the diagnosis supports the billed procedure,
whether different documents refer to the same clinical event despite different wording,
whether explanations in free-text medical notes are consistent with invoices,
and explain issues in natural language.

The value comes from combining document understanding, reasoning across multiple sources, and actionable recommendations, not from simple rule matching alone.
